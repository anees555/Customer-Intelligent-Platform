## Key Business Questions

This project aims to answer a range of business questions by transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights. Some of the key questions include:

* Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
* Which products and product categories generate the highest sales and revenue?
* Which products are frequently purchased and which are underperforming?
* How do sales and customer activity change over time?
* Which sellers contribute the most revenue and maintain the highest customer satisfaction?
* Who are the most valuable customers based on purchase behavior and spending patterns?
* How do customer purchasing patterns vary across different cities and states?
* What factors influence customer reviews and delivery performance?
* Which customers are at risk of churn based on their purchasing history?
* Can customer behavior be leveraged to predict future purchases or customer lifetime value?
