# Exploratory Data Analysis

## Executive KPIs

### Purpose

Executive Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) provide a high-level overview of the overall business performance. These metrics summarize sales, customer activity, operational efficiency, and customer satisfaction, serving as the foundation for subsequent analyses.

### KPI Summary

| KPI | Value |
|------|------:|
| Total Revenue | **15,846,280.17** |
| Total Orders | **98,666** |
| Total Customers | **95,420** |
| Average Order Value | **160.61** |
| Average Delivery Time | **12.50 days** |
| Average Customer Review | **4.11 / 5.00** |
| Repeat Customer Rate | **3.05%** |

### Key Findings

- The platform generated a total revenue of **15.85 million** from **98,666** successfully processed orders.
- A total of **95,420** unique customers completed purchases, indicating a broad customer base.
- The **average order value (AOV)** is **160.61**, representing the average amount spent per order.
- Orders were delivered in an average of **12.5 days**, providing a baseline for evaluating delivery performance.
- Customers reported a high level of satisfaction with an **average review score of 4.11 out of 5**.
- Only **3.05%** of customers placed more than one order, suggesting that the business relies heavily on one-time purchasers.

### Business Insights

- The low repeat customer rate indicates a significant opportunity to improve customer retention through loyalty programs, personalized recommendations, and targeted marketing campaigns.
- Despite the relatively low customer retention, the high average review score suggests that customer satisfaction is generally positive. This implies that encouraging repeat purchases may have a greater impact than improving product quality alone.
- The average order value can serve as a benchmark for measuring the effectiveness of future pricing strategies, promotional campaigns, and cross-selling initiatives.
- Delivery performance and customer satisfaction should be monitored together, as shipping experience is an important factor influencing repeat purchases.

# Sales Analysis

## Overview

The sales analysis examines the overall business performance by evaluating revenue trends, order volume, customer purchasing behavior, payment preferences, operational efficiency, and seasonal sales patterns. The objective is to understand how the business has evolved over time and identify opportunities for improving sales performance.

---

## 1. Monthly Revenue Trend

### Business Question

How has the company's revenue changed over time?

### Purpose

To evaluate business growth, identify periods of rapid expansion or decline, and detect long-term sales trends.

### Key Findings

- Revenue increased steadily throughout 2017, indicating continuous business growth.
- November 2017 generated the highest monthly revenue during the observed period, exceeding **1.17 million**.
- Revenue remained consistently above **1 million** during several months of 2018.
- The extremely low revenue observed in September 2016, December 2016, and September 2018 is due to incomplete data for those months and should not be interpreted as actual business performance.

### Business Insight

The marketplace experienced rapid expansion during 2017 and maintained strong sales performance throughout 2018. The significant increase in revenue during November suggests the impact of seasonal shopping events such as Black Friday.

---

## 2. Monthly Order Trend

### Business Question

How has the number of customer orders changed over time?

### Purpose

To determine whether business growth was driven by increasing customer demand.

### Key Findings

- Order volume closely followed the revenue trend.
- Monthly orders increased from fewer than **1,000** orders in early 2017 to over **7,000** orders during peak periods.
- November 2017 recorded the largest monthly order volume.

### Business Insight

The increase in revenue is primarily associated with an increase in customer orders rather than unusually expensive purchases, indicating healthy marketplace growth.

---

## 3. Average Order Value (AOV)

### Business Question

Has the average spending per order changed over time?

### Purpose

To evaluate customer purchasing behavior and basket size.

### Key Findings

- Average Order Value remained relatively stable throughout the analysis period.
- Monthly AOV generally ranged between **147** and **185**.
- No significant upward or downward trend was observed.

### Business Insight

Although overall revenue increased substantially, customers maintained relatively consistent spending per order. Business growth was therefore driven mainly by increasing transaction volume rather than larger basket sizes.

---

## 4. Delivery Performance Trend

### Business Question

Has delivery performance improved over time?

### Purpose

To evaluate logistics efficiency and delivery operations.

### Key Findings

- Delivery performance improved considerably over time.
- Average delivery time decreased from approximately **15–17 days** during late 2017 and early 2018 to approximately **8 days** by August 2018.
- The unusually high delivery time observed in September 2016 is based on only a few orders and is not representative.

### Business Insight

The continuous reduction in delivery time indicates improvements in logistics operations, which can positively influence customer satisfaction and repeat purchases.

---

# Geographic Analysis

## Overview

The geographic analysis examines customer and seller distribution across different regions of Brazil to understand regional sales performance, marketplace coverage, and delivery efficiency. The objective is to identify high-performing markets, evaluate geographic concentration, and assess operational performance across states and cities.

---

## 1. Revenue by State

### Business Question

Which states generate the highest marketplace revenue?

### Purpose

To identify the states that contribute the largest share of sales revenue and customer orders.

### Key Findings

- **São Paulo (SP)** is the largest market, generating approximately **5.94 million** in revenue from over **41,000 orders**.
- **Rio de Janeiro (RJ)** and **Minas Gerais (MG)** rank second and third, contributing approximately **2.14 million** and **1.86 million** in revenue, respectively.
- Together, **São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Minas Gerais** account for a substantial proportion of the marketplace's total revenue.
- Although states such as **Pará (PA)**, **Paraíba (PB)**, **Rondônia (RO)**, and **Acre (AC)** recorded relatively lower sales volumes, they achieved comparatively higher average order values.

### Business Insight

The marketplace is highly concentrated in Brazil's southeastern region, with São Paulo serving as the primary revenue driver. Smaller states exhibit lower transaction volumes but higher spending per order, indicating potential opportunities for targeted regional expansion.

---

## 2. Revenue by City

### Business Question

Which cities generate the highest revenue?

### Purpose

To identify the cities that contribute most significantly to marketplace sales.

### Key Findings

- **São Paulo city** generated the highest revenue, exceeding **2.17 million** from more than **15,000 orders**.
- **Rio de Janeiro**, **Belo Horizonte**, **Brasília**, and **Curitiba** are the next largest revenue-generating cities.
- Several metropolitan cities consistently appear among the top contributors, reflecting strong customer demand within major urban centers.
- Cities such as **Belém** recorded comparatively higher average order values despite processing fewer orders.

### Business Insight

Sales activity is concentrated in Brazil's major metropolitan areas, suggesting that urban markets remain the primary drivers of marketplace growth and customer demand.

---

## 3. Customer Distribution

### Business Question

How are customers distributed across different states?

### Purpose

To understand the geographic concentration of the customer base.

### Key Findings

- **São Paulo** accounts for approximately **42%** of all customers, making it the marketplace's largest customer base.
- **Rio de Janeiro** and **Minas Gerais** contribute approximately **13%** and **12%** of customers, respectively.
- The southeastern states collectively represent the majority of marketplace customers.
- Northern states such as **Roraima**, **Amapá**, and **Acre** contribute less than **0.1%** of the customer population.

### Business Insight

The customer base is heavily concentrated in economically developed regions of Brazil. Future expansion strategies may focus on increasing customer acquisition in underrepresented northern and northeastern states.

---

## 4. Seller Distribution

### Business Question

How are marketplace sellers distributed geographically?

### Purpose

To evaluate the regional distribution of marketplace sellers.

### Key Findings

- **São Paulo** hosts approximately **60%** of all sellers on the platform.
- **Paraná**, **Minas Gerais**, **Santa Catarina**, and **Rio de Janeiro** collectively account for a significant proportion of the remaining sellers.
- Several states contain very few active sellers, with some represented by only a single marketplace seller.

### Business Insight

The marketplace's supply network is even more geographically concentrated than its customer base. The dominance of São Paulo reflects its role as Brazil's primary commercial and logistics hub, while expanding the seller network in underserved regions could improve marketplace coverage and delivery efficiency.

---

## 5. Delivery Performance by State

### Business Question

How does delivery performance vary across different states?

### Purpose

To compare delivery efficiency across customer locations.

### Key Findings

- **São Paulo** recorded the fastest average delivery time at approximately **8.7 days**.
- **Paraná**, **Minas Gerais**, and **Distrito Federal** also achieved relatively fast deliveries, averaging between **12 and 13 days**.
- Delivery times gradually increase for customers located farther from major logistics centers.
- **Roraima**, **Amapá**, and **Amazonas** experienced the longest average delivery times, ranging from approximately **27 to 30 days**.

### Business Insight

Delivery performance is strongly influenced by geographic location and logistics infrastructure. Customers located near major commercial centers receive significantly faster deliveries, while remote northern states experience considerably longer shipping times. These findings highlight opportunities for optimizing warehouse locations, transportation routes, and regional fulfillment strategies.

---

# Overall Geographic Insights

The geographic analysis demonstrates that both demand and supply are highly concentrated in Brazil's southeastern region, particularly in **São Paulo**, which dominates revenue generation, customer population, seller concentration, and delivery efficiency. Major metropolitan areas serve as the marketplace's primary growth engines, while northern and less populated states contribute relatively smaller sales volumes and experience longer delivery times. These results suggest that maintaining strong operations in core markets while strategically expanding seller networks and logistics infrastructure in underserved regions could improve nationwide marketplace coverage, customer satisfaction, and long-term business growth.
